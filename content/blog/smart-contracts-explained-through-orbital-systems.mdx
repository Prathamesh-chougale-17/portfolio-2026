## Mission premise

Smart contracts behave like orbital bodies. They have gravity, boundaries, and predictable paths. Other systems enter their orbit through calls, events, and signed intent.

## System architecture

The frontend should never treat the wallet as a magic button. It should explain what is being signed, what contract receives it, and what state change is expected.
